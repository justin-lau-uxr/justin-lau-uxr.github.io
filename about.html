A little more about me

For as long as I can remember, I’ve always been a super curious person. This drive for wanting to learn more is one of the main reasons I got interested in the world of UX. I love learning about people; the way they work, live, play, and interact with the things in their everyday lives. This curiosity of wanting to know “what makes someone tick”, and never being afraid to ask why, is what sparked my interest specifically in UX research. Through my work experience I’m also learning what it means to advocate for UXR, and what it takes to help build a team that understands the necessity of research, and is confident in their ability to conduct, interpret, and synthesize UXR to produce data-driven design decisions. Everyday I get to enjoy this process of learning, unlearning, and relearning, and it’s helped shape me into the person I am today!
