CIBC - Life Insurance

Using formative and summative evaluations to inform design direction of the reimagined Life Insurance online experience.

role: lead researcher in a team of 2 designers, 1 strategist, and 1 project manager

methods: Surveys, Unmoderated usability testing

software: Feedback Loop, UserTesting, Figma

timeline: February 2022, 3 months

To comply with my non-disclosure agreement, I have omitted and obfuscated confidential information in this case study. All information in this case study is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of CIBC.
